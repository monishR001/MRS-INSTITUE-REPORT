#  **STUDENTS ENROLLMENT DASHBOARD**

##  **📌 Objective**

Create a Students Enrollment Dashboard in Power BI to track key business metrics and provide actionable insights for decision-makers.

##  **📌 Data Source**

**The dashboard is built using multiple data sources:**

-  Students Personal Details (Excel, SQL, or CSV)

-  Course Details (Course Name, Fees, Date of Joining)

##  **📌 DataSet used**

-  <a href ="https://github.com/monishR001/MRS-INSTITUE-REPORT/blob/main/Student%20Personal%20Details.xlsx">Dataset</a>

##  **📌 Target Users**

**The dashboard is designed for:**

-  Management – To monitor Students Enrollment and Staff efficiency.

-  Finance Department – To track overall revenue and profit trends.

##  **📌 Dashboard Features**

1.✅ Enrollments – A high-level summary of total Enrollments, profit, and key metrics.

2.✅ Year and Monthly wise Enrollments – Line and bar charts to analyze growth over time.

3.✅ Total certified students – Identifying the total certified students

4.✅ Total Fees Amount – Insights into Total fees across different categories.

5.✅ Dynamic Filters – Interactive slicers for Date,city,course.

##  **📌 How to Use**

1.Load the Dataset into Power BI.

2.Transform & Clean Data using Power Query.

3.Create Data Model with proper relationships.

4.Build Visuals for KPIs, trends, and insights.

5.Publish & Share the dashboard.
